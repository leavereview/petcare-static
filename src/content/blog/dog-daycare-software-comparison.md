## What to Look For in Dog Daycare Software

Before comparing specific platforms, understand the essential features:

### Must-Have Features
- **Online booking** - 24/7 reservations without phone calls
- **Capacity management** - Prevent overbooking automatically
- **Vaccination tracking** - Automatic expiry alerts and reminders
- **Payment processing** - Accept cards, process deposits
- **Customer portal** - Self-service for pet parents

### Nice-to-Have Features
- Report cards with photos
- Staff scheduling
- Multi-location support
- Retail/POS integration
- Mobile apps for staff and clients

## Questions to Ask Before Choosing

1. **What's the total cost?** Including setup fees, per-booking fees, and add-ons
2. **How long is the contract?** Month-to-month is safest
3. **What's the learning curve?** Ask for a demo and try it yourself
4. **How's the customer support?** Check reviews specifically about support quality
5. **Can I migrate my data?** Important if switching from another system
