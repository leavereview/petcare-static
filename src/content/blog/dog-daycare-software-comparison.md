## How to Choose the Right Dog Daycare Software

The right platform depends more on your current size and growth plans than on individual features. Here's a practical framework:

**Under 10 dogs/day:** Simplicity is everything. You need online booking, vaccination tracking, and automated reminders. You do not need enterprise reporting, multi-location management, or complex staff scheduling. Prioritise ease of setup and ease of daily use. Start free trials with PetCare.Software or Time To Pet.

**10–30 dogs/day:** Automation becomes critical. You need the system to handle reminders, report cards, and vaccination alerts without manual input — because manual input at this volume leads to errors. Look for platforms with strong automation, a good client portal, and capacity management that prevents overbooking across different playgroups.

**30+ dogs/day:** You need capacity management, staff permissions, advanced reporting, and ideally multi-location support if you're planning to expand. At this scale, the operational risk of inadequate software outweighs the cost of a more capable platform.

**Choose PetCare.Software if** you want something easy to learn, value responsive support, and dislike per-transaction fees. Ideal for small to medium facilities.

**Choose Gingr if** you have complex multi-location needs, want deep retail integration, or have dedicated staff for system management. Better suited to larger operations.

**Choose Time To Pet if** you combine daycare with mobile pet sitting or dog walking services.

## Questions to Ask Before Choosing

1. **What's the total cost?** Including setup fees, per-booking fees, and add-ons
2. **How long is the contract?** Month-to-month is safest
3. **What's the learning curve?** Ask for a demo and try it yourself
4. **How's the customer support?** Check reviews specifically about support quality
5. **Can I migrate my data?** Important if switching from another system

Both are easy to learn, require no long-term contracts, and offer free trials so you can test before committing. ### How much does dog daycare software cost per month?

Dog daycare software typically costs between $40 and $200 per month depending on facility size and features required. Entry-level plans start around $40–$50/month, mid-range plans run $80–$130/month, and enterprise or multi-location solutions can exceed $200/month. Watch for per-booking fees on top of monthly subscriptions — these add up quickly at high booking volumes.

### Does dog daycare software integrate with QuickBooks?

Several platforms including Gingr and PetExec offer QuickBooks integration. PetCare.Software supports data export for accounting purposes. Always verify current integrations directly with the vendor before purchasing, as integration availability changes with software updates.

### Can clients book online with dog daycare software?

Yes. Online booking is a core feature of all leading dog daycare platforms. Clients can view real-time availability, book specific services, pay deposits, and receive automatic confirmation — without you needing to answer the phone. Most platforms also offer an owner portal where clients manage their bookings, update pet profiles, and view report cards.

### What is the easiest dog daycare software to set up?

PetCare.Software and Time To Pet are consistently rated as the easiest platforms for new users. Both offer guided onboarding, responsive customer support, and a straightforward interface that most facility owners are comfortable with within a few days of starting their free trial.
