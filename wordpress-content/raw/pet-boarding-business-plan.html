Risk Planning

\n
    \n
  • Demand variability: Use daycare memberships to stabilize weekday revenue; promote stay-and-play bundles in off-peak months.
    • \n
  • Health outbreaks: Strict vaccine/illness policies, sanitation SOPs, isolation room, and customer comms templates.
    • \n
  • Staffing: Cross-train, offer retention bonuses, maintain a PT bench of on-call attendants.
    • \n
  • Competition: Differentiate with webcams, enrichment reports, later pickup windows, or transport add-ons.
    • \n
  • Regulatory: Keep digital records (vaccines, incidents, cleaning logs) for inspections via kennel management software.
