What Is Dog Boarding Software?

\n

Dog boarding software is a specialized tool designed to manage boarding facilities efficiently. Unlike generic booking apps, it includes pet-specific features such as vaccination records, owner portals, and kennel/run management.

\n

Key features typically include:

\n
    \n
  • Online booking & payments — clients can reserve and pay from anywhere.
    • \n
  • Pet profiles — vaccination history, dietary needs, notes for staff.
    • \n
  • Owner portal — self-service for bookings, documents, and payments.
    • \n
  • Staff scheduling — shift management and staff-to-dog ratio monitoring.
    • \n
  • Automated reminders — email or SMS for bookings, vaccinations, or pickups.
    • \n
  • Boarding run management — assign pets to rooms or suites, avoid double bookings.
    • \n
\n

Some solutions extend into daycare, grooming, or training — others focus on pure boarding. Your choice should match your services and growth plans.

Common Pitfalls When Choosing Boarding Software

\n

Even with clear feature lists, mistakes happen during the selection process. Avoid these common pitfalls:

\n

1. Ignoring Scalability

\n

A simple booking tool may work for a 10-run kennel but break down if you expand. Choose software that can handle growth — or plan to migrate later (which is costly).

\n

2. Overlooking Mobile Usability

\n

Your staff and clients need mobile access. If the software isn't mobile-friendly, booking and check-in become cumbersome.

\n

3. Not Testing Customer Support

\n

Read reviews about support quality. Slow or unresponsive support can cripple your operation during peak seasons.

\n

4. Hidden Fees

\n

Some platforms advertise low base prices but charge per transaction, per location, or for essential features like reminders. Clarify all fees before committing.

\n

5. Skipping the Trial

\n

Most platforms offer free trials. Use them to test workflows, interface speed, and reporting. Don't commit without hands-on experience.

Frequently Asked Questions About Dog Boarding Software

\n\n

What is dog boarding software?

\n

Dog boarding software is a specialized management platform designed for pet boarding facilities that automates reservations, handles check-ins/check-outs, manages vaccination records, processes payments, and tracks kennel availability in real-time. It replaces manual booking systems with digital solutions that improve efficiency and customer experience.

\n\n

How does online booking work for boarding facilities?

\n

Online booking for dog boarding allows pet owners to view available dates, select kennel types, add services (like grooming or training), and complete their reservation 24/7 through your website. The system automatically updates availability, sends confirmation emails, and can require deposits or full payment at booking to reduce no-shows.

\n\n

Can boarding software manage deposits and payments?

\n

Yes, modern boarding software integrates with payment processors to handle deposits, full payments, refunds, and recurring charges. You can set deposit requirements (like 50% upfront), process credit card payments securely, generate invoices, and automatically charge cancellation fees based on your policies.

\n\n

What vaccination tracking features should I look for?

\n

Essential vaccination tracking features include digital storage of vaccination records, automatic expiration alerts, boarding approval workflows that verify current vaccinations before check-in, and reminders sent to pet owners before vaccines expire. The best systems can import records directly from veterinary clinics and require proof of rabies, DHPP, and bordetella vaccinations.

\n\n

How does boarding software prevent overbooking?

\n

Boarding software prevents overbooking through real-time kennel inventory management that automatically updates availability across all booking channels (website, phone, walk-in). When a kennel is booked, it's immediately marked as unavailable, and the system can also account for blocked dates, maintenance periods, and different kennel types to ensure accurate capacity management.
