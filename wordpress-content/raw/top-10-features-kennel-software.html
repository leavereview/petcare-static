10 Must-Have Features of a Kennel Management System

\n\n

1. Online Booking

\n

Pet parents expect to book online 24/7, not just during business hours. The system should allow secure reservations, deposits, and package purchases.

\n\n

2. Vaccination Tracking

\n

Vaccination compliance is critical. Look for automated reminders and easy uploads of certificates to avoid manual chasing.

\n\n

3. Owner Portal

\n

A self-service portal reduces phone calls and admin. Owners can update records, upload documents, and pay invoices online.

\n\n

4. Automated Reminders

\n

Email or SMS reminders for bookings, check-ins, and payments cut no-shows and improve customer satisfaction.

\n\n

5. Pet Profiles

\n

Detailed pet records should include health history, feeding schedules, medication, and behavior notes for safer care.

\n\n

6. Staff Scheduling

\n

Even in small kennels, staff schedules matter. Built-in scheduling tools ensure coverage and help balance workloads.

\n\n

7. Reporting & Analytics

\n

Basic reports on occupancy, revenue, and trends help you make better decisions and plan growth.

\n\n

8. Payment Integration

\n

Integrated billing and payments streamline the process for both owners and staff. No more chasing invoices.

\n\n

9. Mobile Access

\n

Cloud-based, mobile-friendly access means you can manage bookings from anywhere, anytime.

\n\n

10. Scalability

\n

Choose software that grows with you. Whether you expand into boarding, daycare, or catteries, your system should handle it without disruption.
