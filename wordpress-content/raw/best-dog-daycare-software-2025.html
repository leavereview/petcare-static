What Is Dog Daycare Software?

\n

Dog daycare software is a specialized management system designed to handle daily daycare operations efficiently. Unlike generic booking tools, it includes features tailored to pet care:

\n
    \n
  • Online booking & payments: Clients can schedule daycare visits, buy packages, and pay securely.
    • \n
  • Pet profiles: Store vaccination records, dietary needs, playgroup preferences.
    • \n
  • Owner portal: Pet parents can update details, sign waivers, and view invoices.
    • \n
  • Staff & playgroup scheduling: Assign dogs to groups, track ratios, and manage shifts.
    • \n
  • Upsell management: Add grooming, training, or retail to daycare bookings.
    • \n
  • Automated reminders: Reduce no-shows and keep clients engaged.

Frequently Asked Questions About Dog Daycare Software

\n\n

What features should dog daycare software include?

\n

Essential features include online booking with package support, automated vaccination tracking and reminders, staff scheduling with ratio enforcement, client self-service portals, mobile accessibility for both staff and clients, and integrated payment processing. The best systems also offer playgroup management, reporting dashboards, and upsell capabilities for services like grooming or training.

\n\n

How does daycare software handle daily attendance tracking?

\n

Modern daycare software provides quick check-in/check-out systems that can work via mobile devices, tablets, or desktop computers. Staff can scan QR codes, tap pet profiles, or use bulk check-in features during busy morning drop-offs. The system automatically updates attendance records, tracks which dogs are in which playgroups, monitors staff-to-dog ratios in real-time, and maintains a complete history of visits for billing and reporting purposes.

\n\n

Can parents receive updates and photos through the software?

\n

Yes, most quality daycare software includes parent portal features where pet parents can log in to see real-time updates, photos, and report cards about their dog's day. Staff can quickly upload photos and add notes about behavior, meals, or activities. Some systems also send automated text or email notifications for check-in, check-out, and special updates, keeping parents connected throughout the day without constant phone calls.

\n\n

What's the difference between daycare and boarding software?

\n

While both types manage pet care bookings, daycare software focuses on recurring daily visits, playgroup management, quick check-ins, and high-volume daily operations. Boarding software emphasizes overnight stays, run/kennel assignments, feeding schedules, and medication tracking. The best platforms like PetCare.Software combine both capabilities, letting you manage daycare and boarding services in one system without switching between different tools.

\n\n

How does online booking work for dog daycares?

\n

Online booking allows pet parents to view your daycare's availability in real-time, select dates for drop-off, choose any add-on services like grooming or training, and complete payment — all through a web portal or mobile app. The system automatically checks vaccination requirements, enforces cancellation policies, sends confirmation emails, and updates your schedule. Parents can also purchase daycare packages (like 10-visit passes) or set up recurring reservations for regular weekly visits, reducing administrative work for your staff.
