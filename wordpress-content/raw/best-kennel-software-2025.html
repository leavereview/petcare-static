What Is Kennel Software?

\n

Kennel software is a specialized management tool designed for boarding facilities, daycares, and multi-service pet businesses. Unlike generic booking systems, it includes features tailored to pet care:

\n
    \n
  • Online booking & payments — accept reservations and deposits 24/7.
    • \n
  • Pet records — vaccination history, allergies, diet notes.
    • \n
  • Owner portal — pet parents can update details, upload documents, and see invoices.
    • \n
  • Staff scheduling — manage ratios, shifts, and workload.
    • \n
  • Reminders — automated SMS/email to reduce no-shows and keep clients engaged.
    • \n
  • Multi-location support — for businesses operating more than one facility.
    • \n
\n

Some solutions also include grooming, training, or retail modules. Others focus purely on boarding and daycare. Choosing the right one depends on your size, budget, and services offered.

Frequently Asked Questions About Kennel Software

\n\n

What is kennel software and who needs it?

\n

Kennel software is a specialized management system designed for pet boarding facilities, kennels, and multi-service pet businesses. It automates booking, scheduling, client communication, and record-keeping. Any business that boards pets overnight—from small kennels with 10 runs to large facilities with multiple locations—benefits from kennel software. It's particularly valuable for businesses that want to offer online booking, track vaccination records, manage staff schedules, and reduce administrative workload.

\n\n

How much does kennel software typically cost?

\n

Kennel software pricing typically ranges from £29-£69 per month ($35-$85 USD) for basic plans, with more comprehensive solutions starting around $100-$150 per month. Most vendors offer tiered pricing based on the number of users, locations, or pets managed. Some platforms like PetCare.Software offer customized pricing tailored to your specific needs. Factor in setup fees (often $100-$500) and training costs. Most vendors offer free trials lasting 14-30 days, allowing you to test the software risk-free before committing.

\n\n

What are the essential features of kennel management software?

\n

Essential features include online booking and scheduling, pet profile management with vaccination tracking, automated email and SMS reminders, payment processing with deposits, staff scheduling tools, and a client portal where pet owners can manage bookings and view invoices. Additional valuable features include waitlist management, multi-location support, reporting and analytics, integration with accounting software, and mobile access. The best kennel software combines these features in an intuitive interface that reduces training time and administrative overhead.

\n\n

Can kennel software integrate with my existing systems?

\n

Most modern kennel software offers integration capabilities with popular business tools. Common integrations include payment processors (Stripe, Square, PayPal), accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero), email marketing platforms (Mailchimp), and calendar systems (Google Calendar, Outlook). Some platforms like PetCare.Software offer API access for custom integrations. When evaluating software, ask vendors specifically about integrations with tools you currently use, and request documentation or demo of the integration process to ensure it meets your workflow requirements.

\n\n

How long does it take to set up kennel software?

\n

Setup time varies based on business complexity and data migration needs. Basic setup—creating your account, adding services, and configuring booking rules—typically takes 2-4 hours. Migrating existing client and pet records can add several days, depending on volume and data quality. Most vendors provide onboarding support, training materials, and implementation guides. Plan for 1-2 weeks of parallel operation (running old and new systems simultaneously) to ensure smooth transition. Staff training usually requires 2-3 sessions of 1-2 hours each. Full adoption typically occurs within 30-45 days.

Is kennel software worth it for small facilities?

\n

Yes. Even kennels with fewer than 20 runs benefit from automated booking, vaccination tracking, and reminders. The time saved often outweighs the cost.

\n

Can kennel software manage multiple locations?

\n

Yes. Platforms like PetCare.Software and Gingr support multi-location reporting and staff role permissions.

\n

What's the difference between kennel software and dog boarding software?

\n

Kennel software focuses on facility management, while dog boarding software often overlaps but may include broader client services. Many businesses use the terms interchangeably.

\n

Which kennel software is best for daycares?

\n

Dog daycare software overlaps with kennel tools, but daycare-focused platforms emphasize scheduling, staff management, and frequent communication with pet parents.

Is there kennel software for catteries?
